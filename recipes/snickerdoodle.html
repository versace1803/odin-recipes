Mrs. Siggs Snickerdoodle Recipe

This recipe tells you step by step, exactly how to create the perfect snickerdoodle cookies that your family will love.

For this recipe you will need:

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Cinnamon-Sugar(2 tablespoons white sugar and 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Mix sugar, butter, shortening, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until creamy.
  3. Whisk flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt together in a seperate bowl.
  4. Slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined.
  5. Shape the dough into walnut sized balls.
  6. Make the cinnamon-sugar by combined cinnamon and sugar in a bag and shaking.
  7. Place the balls of dough into the cinnamon-sugar, and roll until coated.
  8. Place the balls two inches apart on a baking sheet.
  9. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven until set, switching the racks halfway through, which should be around 8-10 minutes.
  10. Remove the cookies from the oven and place them on a wire rack until cooled.
